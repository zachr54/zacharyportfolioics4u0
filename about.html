About Me

A closer look at my school life, hobbies, teams, and achievements.

My Story

Hi, I'm Zach! I am a Grade 11 student passionate about academics, Math, Computer Science, athletics, and the arts.

What makes me the person I am today is my desire to constantly challenge myself in as large variety of subjects. Whether I'm working with Math and Computer Science, lifting weights, playing on a sports team, or performing dance and music, I thrive in challenges and work through struggles. These experienes have taught me the value of discipline, humillity, teamwork, desire, and expression. This allows me to approach my life in a strong, unique way.

Arts

Classical Guitar

Music is an incredibly valuable part of my life. I practice weekly and work towards perfecting classical music pieces and performing them at the Provincial level. This has taught me the power of patience and focus. It has also caused me to value discipline and consistency over bursts of motivation.

Cultural Dance

Every Friday and Sunday, I participate in dance practices, connecting me to my Bulgarian heritage and teaching me to perform on stage. It has allowed me to appreciate my Bulgarian roots and has aided me to develop confidence in public settings.

Teams

🏉 Rugby

Competing on the pitch has taught me resilience, teamwork, and discipline in a unique way that many other activities cannot. It has made me a more determined, tough-minded individual who isn't afraid to take hits and keep moving forward.

Robotics (Mackenzie Lyons)

Designing, building, and programming a robot as part of the Mackenzie FIRST Tech Challenge team has allowed me to develop critical thinking and Engineering-focused skills.

Powerlifting

For me, powerlifting isn't like any other sport. It teaches me to hold myself accountable to what I wish to accomplish and provides an environment where luck is not a factor; only discipline and training.
